Examples of the proof discovery process

A YouTube series of live problem-solving created by Tim Gowers. We would be delighted to link to similar videos made by others: please let us know of any that you know of, or have made yourself.

We are trying to formulate a notion of "motivated proof". Roughly speaking, this means a proof that does not involve rabbits being drawn out of hats. While we do not yet have a precise notion yet, we do have several examples.

A list of challenge problems

The style of problem that we are trying to solve is a little different from the benchmark problems one finds in typical databases, in that the problems are more like the easier problems that one might find in a mathematics undergraduate problem set. So we would like to create our own collection of problems and use it to measure our progress. We have made a start on this and would welcome further suggestions.
